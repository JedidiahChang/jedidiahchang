About me

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

Hello, Welcome to my website! I am a freelancing traveling Photographer/Cinematographer that has been behind the camera for more than 8 years. I was born in Minnesota where I am also based out of. In highschool, I was a yearbook editor for two years, where my intrest for cameras sparked.

I went to the University of Wisconsin Milwaukee for a bachelors in Film. From there, I worked for a small film company primarily editing for them. Now, I travel with my girlfriend (who is also a photographer) around the world taking pictures of the worlds wonders. Also, I do: wedding, senior, baby, birthday, etc. Oh yeah, and my name is Jedidiah Chang (張杰).
